## What the rule addresses

The regular rate is a calculation, not necessarily the employee's stated hourly rate. Depending on the rule and payment type, it can include commissions, piece-rate earnings, and nondiscretionary bonuses before pricing overtime or certain premiums.

Different payment forms may require different allocation periods or divisors. A single blended shortcut can obscure those distinctions.

## What to inspect

- Inventory all remuneration paid for the period, including later bonus or commission true-ups.
- Identify which amounts may be excluded and document the basis for each exclusion.
- Match each payment type to the governing allocation and divisor method.
- Confirm that later-paid incentive amounts trigger any required recalculation.

The correct method depends on the payment's substance, timing, and jurisdiction.

## What the pay system does

Sales plans commonly combine a percentage of gross, flat amounts per unit, bonuses, packs, and a draw against future commissions. The writing should define how each component is calculated, when it becomes earned, when it is paid, and what happens after a cancellation or unwind.

Federal and California commissioned-employee overtime rules use different tests. Coverage cannot be inferred from the job title or from the presence of some commission pay alone.

## What to inspect

- Read the signed plan and every amendment in effect during the period reviewed.
- Reconcile the plan's earned trigger with pay statements, deal funding, cancellations, and chargebacks.
- Test exemption prerequisites using the records and measurement periods each rule requires.
- Separate recoverable advances from compensation allocated to working time and rest periods.

The details of the plan and the pay-period records control the analysis; these checkpoints do not determine an individual result.

## What the pay system does

F&I plans may combine a stated salary or draw with reserve, product, penetration, and performance components. Whether a component functions as a commission, bonus, advance, or guaranteed salary depends on the plan and how payroll applies it.

Section 7(i) can be relevant, but the establishment, regular-rate, commission-share, and representative-period requirements still need support in the actual records. A separate salary-basis theory has its own conditions.

## What to inspect

- Map every pay component to its calculation, earning event, and payment date.
- Preserve the representative-period and workweek records used for any exemption position.
- Check whether lean-period reconciliations reduce a purported guaranteed salary.
- Trace product cancellations and funding reversals to the written chargeback terms.

These checks organize the factual record; they do not establish exemption status or the treatment of a particular payment.

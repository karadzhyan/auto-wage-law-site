## What the pay system does

“Support staff” can cover parts-counter employees, porters, detailers, BDC agents, shuttle drivers, and office employees whose duties and pay methods differ substantially. Some receive hourly wages; others receive unit, appointment, or performance incentives.

Exemption and piece-rate questions therefore belong to the individual role and plan, not to the department label. Contractor-supplied detail or transport crews may also require a separate classification and client-employer review.

## What to inspect

- Inventory each role, employer entity, work location, duties, and pay method.
- Capture opening, lot-preparation, shuttle, closing, and remote-customer-contact time.
- Identify incentive pay tied to units, appointments, or sales and how it appears on statements.
- Review required uniforms, devices, mileage, tools, and safety equipment.

The same department can contain materially different analyses, so conclusions should follow role-specific records.

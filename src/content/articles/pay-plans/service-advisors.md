## What the pay system does

Advisor plans often blend salary or hourly pay with labor sales, parts sales, customer-satisfaction bonuses, and service-contract incentives. The mix can change from one pay period to the next, while lane-opening, closing, and customer-contact work continues on the clock.

The federal dealership exemption for service advisors does not automatically decide California overtime, break, wage-statement, or commission questions.

## What to inspect

- Identify every pay component and the transaction or event that triggers it.
- Compare scheduled, recorded, and actually worked time at opening, closing, and during customer follow-up.
- Test federal and state exemption requirements separately.
- Review how reversals, warranty decisions, and service-contract cancellations affect previously credited pay.

A role label is not a substitute for the governing duties, pay plan, records, and jurisdiction.

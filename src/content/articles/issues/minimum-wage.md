## What the rule addresses

Incentive plans can produce strong total earnings while leaving questions about how specific working time was compensated. Federal and California minimum-wage methods do not always evaluate that record the same way, and piece-rate work adds separate statutory mechanics.

The applicable floor may also vary by year and locality. A site-wide figure should never replace the rate that governed the employee's location and date.

## What to inspect

- Reconcile every recorded work hour with the compensation assigned to the same period.
- Separate flags, commissions, bonuses, advances, guarantees, and hourly wages.
- Use the applicable state and local rates for each date and location.
- Check whether a piece-rate plan separately addresses rest and nonproductive time.

The calculation is an orientation exercise until the complete time and payroll records are aligned.

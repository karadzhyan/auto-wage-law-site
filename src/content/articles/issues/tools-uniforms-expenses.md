## What the rule addresses

Dealership work can require hand tools, diagnostic equipment, uniforms, protective gear, personal phones, mileage, or remote-work resources. California reimbursement law and the wage orders address different parts of that expense picture.

The wage-order hand-tool provision includes an earnings threshold, but the governing wage order and the relevant measurement facts should be established before applying it. Shop equipment and ordinary hand tools should not be treated as interchangeable categories.

## What to inspect

- Inventory every item employees are required or practically expected to supply.
- Identify the governing wage order and the employee's earnings over the relevant periods.
- Separate hand tools from diagnostic subscriptions, scan tools, lifts, safety equipment, and uniforms.
- Review phone, mileage, and other necessary-business-expense reimbursement methods.

Expense treatment depends on necessity, employer direction, the governing order, and the actual reimbursement record.

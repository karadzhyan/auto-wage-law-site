## What the rule addresses

Dealership pay can implicate several distinct overtime paths: the federal dealership exemption, the federal retail commissioned-employee exemption, white-collar rules, and state overtime law. Each path has its own duties, pay, timing, and record requirements.

A federal exemption does not necessarily supply a parallel state exemption, and a job title alone does not resolve coverage.

## What to inspect

- Identify the exact exemption being relied on for each role and jurisdiction.
- Match the employee's actual duties and compensation to every element of that test.
- Keep the workweek, pay-period, and representative-period calculations distinct.
- Re-run the analysis when duties or pay-plan components change.

An exemption analysis is fact-specific and should be confirmed against current law and complete records.
